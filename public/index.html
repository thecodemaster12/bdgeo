Welcome to Bangladesh GeoAPI

A free and open API providing geographical data of Bangladesh, including divisions, districts, and upazilas.

Documentation

Base URL: https://bdgeo.vercel.app/

Namespace: /api

Available Endpoints:

  • /api/divisions - Get all divisions
  • /api/districts - Get all districts
  • /api/upazilas - Get all upazilas
  • /api/division/{division_name} - Get districts of a specific division
  • /api/district/{district_name} - Get upazilas of a specific district
